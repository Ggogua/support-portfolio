# Ticket Responses: Customer Communication Templates

## 📋 Background

| **Company** | Vezir |
| **Role** | Customer Support |
| **Period** | Dec 2022 – Dec 2023 |

---

## 🎯 The Goal

When handling customer tickets, clear and professional communication was essential. I developed standardized response templates to:

- Respond quickly and consistently
- Set proper expectations
- Keep customers informed
- Reduce repeat inquiries
- Maintain professional tone

---

## 📝 Response Templates

### Template 1: Acknowledgment (Initial Response)

**When to use:** First response to a new ticket

```
Hello [Customer Name],

Thank you for reaching out. I've received your ticket and I'm looking into it.

I'll review the details and get back to you within [timeframe] with an update.

If you have any additional information to share, please feel free to add it here.

Best regards,
Giorgi
Support Team
```

---

### Template 2: Investigation in Progress

**When to use:** When you need more time to investigate

```
Hello [Customer Name],

Thank you for your patience. I'm still investigating the issue you reported.

I've cross-referenced the logs and found that the transaction is currently being reviewed. I've escalated this to our technical team for further analysis.

I'll follow up with you as soon as I have an update, likely within [timeframe].

Best regards,
Giorgi
Support Team
```

---

### Template 3: Escalated to Development Team

**When to use:** When issue needs developer attention

```
Hello [Customer Name],

Thank you for your patience while I investigated this.

I've identified that this issue requires attention from our development team. I've created a ticket (JIRA: [TICKET-ID]) and they are now looking into it.

I'll keep you updated on their progress. Estimated resolution time is [timeframe].

Best regards,
Giorgi
Support Team
```

---

### Template 4: Escalated to Provider

**When to use:** When issue is on provider side

```
Hello [Customer Name],

Thank you for your patience.

After reviewing the logs, I've identified that this issue is related to a third-party provider. I've escalated this to their support team with ticket #[PROVIDER-TICKET-ID].

I'm monitoring their response and will update you as soon as I hear back. Provider resolution typically takes [timeframe].

Best regards,
Giorgi
Support Team
```

---

### Template 5: Resolution Confirmation

**When to use:** After issue is fixed

```
Hello [Customer Name],

Great news! The issue you reported has been resolved.

What happened:
- The round was stuck due to [brief explanation]
- Our team has manually corrected the transaction
- Your account has been updated

You should now see the correct balance reflected. Could you please confirm on your end?

If you have any further questions, I'm happy to help.

Best regards,
Giorgi
Support Team
```

---

### Template 6: Provider Fix Confirmation

**When to use:** After provider resolves the issue

```
Hello [Customer Name],

Good news! The third-party provider has resolved the issue on their end.

I've verified that all affected rounds have been processed correctly. Your account should now show the correct balance.

Please let me know if you see any remaining issues.

Best regards,
Giorgi
Support Team
```

---

### Template 7: No Issue Found

**When to use:** When investigation shows no problem

```
Hello [Customer Name],

Thank you for your patience while I looked into this.

After reviewing the logs, I found that:
- Round [ID] was processed successfully at [timestamp]
- The bet and payout amounts match across all systems
- No discrepancies were found

If you're still seeing an issue, could you please provide a screenshot of what you're seeing? That will help me investigate further.

Best regards,
Giorgi
Support Team
```

---

### Template 8: Follow-up Reminder

**When to use:** When waiting for customer response

```
Hello [Customer Name],

I'm following up on this ticket. I haven't heard back from you since my last message on [date].

Is this issue still ongoing, or has it been resolved? If you're still experiencing problems, please let me know and I'll continue investigating.

If I don't hear back within [timeframe], I'll assume this is resolved and close the ticket.

Best regards,
Giorgi
Support Team
```

---

### Template 9: Closing Ticket

**When to use:** After resolution confirmation

```
Hello [Customer Name],

Since I haven't heard back from you, I'll go ahead and close this ticket.

If the issue reoccurs or you have any other questions, please feel free to open a new ticket.

Thank you for your patience throughout this process.

Best regards,
Giorgi
Support Team
```

---

### Template 10: Urgent/Outage Notification

**When to use:** During system outages or widespread issues

```
Hello [Customer Name],

We're currently experiencing a [issue type] that is affecting some users. Our team is actively working on a fix.

I'll update this ticket as soon as the issue is resolved. In the meantime, I appreciate your patience.

No need to reply to this message unless you have additional details to share.

Best regards,
Giorgi
Support Team
```

## 💡 Best Practices I Followed

| Practice                | Why It Matters                            |
| ----------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| **Acknowledge quickly** | Sets expectation, reduces anxiety         |
| **Be transparent**      | Customers appreciate honesty about delays |
| **Set clear timelines** | Reduces follow-up inquiries               |
| **Use simple language** | Avoid technical jargon                    |
| **Stay professional**   | Maintain positive relationship            |
| **Follow up**           | Ensures issues are truly resolved         |
| **Close properly**      | Keeps ticket queue clean                  |

---

## 📋 Quick Reference: When to Use Which Template

| Situation                    | Template to Use            |
| ---------------------------- | -------------------------- |
| First response to new ticket | Acknowledgment             |
| Need more investigation time | Investigation in Progress  |
| Developer needed             | Escalated to Dev Team      |
| Provider issue               | Escalated to Provider      |
| Issue fixed                  | Resolution Confirmation    |
| Provider fixed issue         | Provider Fix Confirmation  |
| No problem found             | No Issue Found             |
| Waiting for customer reply   | Follow-up Reminder         |
| No response after follow-up  | Closing Ticket             |
| System-wide issue            | Urgent/Outage Notification |
